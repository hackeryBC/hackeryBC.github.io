List of What We Recycle

The following is acceptable material in our recycling program. Items can be working or not. Please call ahead before dropping off large quantities.

  • Computers - complete or in parts
  • Laptops - complete or in parts
  • LCDs - Computer or TV
  • Motherboards & Circuit Board
  • Computer cables & Wires
  • Keyboards & Mice
  • Network Equipment
  • Printers & Scanners
  • Game Consoles
  • Cell Phones
  • UPS - Uninterruptible Power Supplies
  • Home Electronics (Stereos, DVD player etc)
  • Rechargable Batteries
  • CRT Monitors
  • CD & DVDs

    • We DO NOT accept the following



  • Old Style TVs
  • Kitchen Appliances (Microwaves, Coffee Machines etc)
  • Toner and Ink cartridges
  • Photocopiers
  • Smoke Detectors
  • PCB containing light ballasts

    • If you are unsure if we accept the material you want to recycle, please Contact Us. If you want to know more about our recycling program please click here.
