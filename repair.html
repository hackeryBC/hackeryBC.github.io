Computer Repair

The Hackery has a simple philosophy: if we can't repair it, we won't charge you! You can expect a detailed explanation of what is wrong with your computer, and a down-to-earth opinion of what should be done.

We offer free diagnostic testing for flat screen monitors or any Mac or PC laptop or computer. We work with any operating system version from Linux, Mac, and Windows. Defective hardware will be replaced with affordable parts from our scrap yard, or we can special order new parts if necessary.

Our shop rate is $60 per hour + tax, with a 1 hour minimum on any job.
